About Me

Greetings! I'm Mohmad Yaqoob, a Computer Science enthusiast currently immersed in the captivating challenges of my third year at NIT Silchar. Proudly hailing from the stunning landscapes of Ladakh.
As a dedicated Computer Science student, I find joy in unraveling the complexities of technology and am passionate about leveraging my skills to make a positive impact. My academic journey has been a thrilling exploration of the world of algorithms, programming languages, and innovative solutions.
